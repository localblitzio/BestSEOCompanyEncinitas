Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept payment for better rankings?

No. Digital Rankings maintains complete editorial independence. We do not accept payment for rankings, reviews, or placement. Our rankings are based solely on our research methodology.

How can my agency be included in your rankings?

We continuously research agencies in our coverage areas. You can suggest your agency using the contact form above, but inclusion is based solely on our evaluation criteria, not on requests or submissions.

I disagree with my ranking. How can I appeal?

We welcome feedback about our rankings. Please contact us with specific information about why you believe your evaluation should be reconsidered. We'll review your concerns and respond with our findings.

How often are rankings updated?

We conduct major ranking updates annually, with quarterly reviews for significant changes. If agency circumstances change substantially, we may update individual listings between scheduled reviews.
